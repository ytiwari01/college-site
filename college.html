About LIET

We are one of the largest, most diverse Engineering College in the Rajasthan with over 2500 students in campus.

Laxmi Devi Institute of Engineering & Technology (LIET), Alwar (Rajasthan), established by Marut Nandan Educational Society (MNES) in the year 2001 is affiliated to the Bikaner Technical University, Bikaner (Rajasthan) and approved by Govt. of Rajasthan & AICTE, Ministry of HRD, New Delhi. LIET means to produce Laborious, Intelligent, Educated and Talented Engineers. It has a vision to create a center of excellence for the Technical Education, Research & Development and Consultancy.
